Privacy Policy

How Hunter handles website data.

This site is intentionally simple. We only want a minimal, clear privacy posture.

1. Website usage

Hunter may collect limited technical information that is necessary to operate, secure, and improve the website. This can include basic request metadata, browser information, and consent preferences stored in your browser.

2. Cookies

Hunter uses a consent banner to record whether you accepted or rejected additional cookies. That preference is stored locally in your browser. If optional analytics or similar tools are added later, they should only run after consent is granted.

3. Contact submissions

If you contact Hunter by email, the information you send may be retained for business communication, diligence, partnership, or support follow-up.

4. Third-party services

If Hunter later adds embedded services, analytics, scheduling tools, or forms, this policy should be updated to describe those providers and their data handling.

5. Your choices

You can reject additional cookies through the website banner. You can also clear browser storage to reset your preference.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact info@hunter-mvp.com.
